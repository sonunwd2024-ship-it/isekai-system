⚔️ HOW RANKINGS WORK
Everything you need to know about competing
⚡
XP SCORE
Earn XP by completing tasks: S-rank = 100 XP, A-rank = 60, B-rank = 30, C-rank = 10. Focus sessions give +50 XP. Habits give +20 XP each.
🔥
STREAK SCORE
Your longest habit streak ever recorded. Keep completing habits daily to build your streak and climb the rankings!
✅
QUESTS SCORE
Total number of tasks you have ever completed across daily, weekly, and monthly quests. Every completed quest counts!
🔄
LIVE UPDATES
The leaderboard updates in real-time via Firebase. Your score updates automatically 3 seconds after you earn XP.
🛡️
FAIR PLAY
Each user can only update their own score. Firebase rules prevent any cheating — your rank is earned, never faked!
🖼️
PROFILE PICTURE
Upload a profile photo in your Profile page and it will appear on the podium and leaderboard for all players to see!
